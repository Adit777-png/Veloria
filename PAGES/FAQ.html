FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about Veloria. We're here to help you find the perfect gown.

How Can We Help You?

Find answers to our most commonly asked questions below. Can't find what you're looking for? Our customer care team is always ready to assist.

Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take?

+

Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days within the continental US. Express shipping (2-3 business days) and overnight options are also available at checkout. International shipping times vary by destination, typically 10-15 business days.

Do you offer free shipping?

+

Yes! We offer complimentary standard shipping on all orders over $200 within the United States. For orders under $200, standard shipping is $15. International shipping rates vary by location.

Can I track my order?

+

Absolutely! Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also log into your account to view real-time tracking information for all your orders.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

+

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for unworn, unaltered items with original tags attached. All items must be in their original condition. Return shipping is free for US customers. Once we receive your return, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

How do I exchange an item?

+

To exchange an item, simply initiate a return through your account and place a new order for your desired size or style. We'll process your refund as soon as we receive the original item, and your new order will ship immediately.

Are sale items returnable?

+

Yes! Sale items follow the same return policy as regular-priced items. They must be returned within 30 days in unworn, unaltered condition with original tags attached.

Sizing & Fit

How do I find my perfect size?

+

Each product page includes a detailed size guide with measurements. We recommend measuring yourself and comparing to our size chart. If you're between sizes, we suggest sizing up for a more comfortable fit. Our customer care team is also available to provide personalized sizing advice.

Do you offer custom alterations?

+

While we don't offer in-house alterations, we can recommend trusted tailors in your area. Many of our gowns are designed with alterations in mind, featuring extra seam allowances for easy adjustments.

What if my dress doesn't fit?

+

No worries! You can return or exchange any unworn, unaltered dress within 30 days. We offer free return shipping for US customers, making size exchanges completely hassle-free.

Product Care

How should I care for my Veloria gown?

+

Each gown comes with specific care instructions on the label. Generally, we recommend dry cleaning for our luxury pieces to maintain their beauty and longevity. Some items may be hand-washable - always check the care label first.

How do I store my gown?

+

Store your gown in a breathable garment bag in a cool, dry place. Avoid plastic bags which can trap moisture. For heavily beaded or embellished pieces, lay flat when possible to maintain their shape.

Payment & Security

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are processed securely through encrypted payment gateways.

Is my payment information secure?

+

Absolutely. We never store your credit card details on our servers - all payment processing is handled securely by certified payment processors.

Do you offer payment plans?

+

Yes!
